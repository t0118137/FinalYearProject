  • Government announced suspension to the Extradition Bill"

    At a press conference, the Hong Kong Government has announced the suspension of the Extradition Bill. But on a legal level, the word "suspension" does not have any legal binding power which causing Hongkongers still depressed about this announcement. On the same day, a teenager committed suicide. He was holding up a sign about the Anti Extradition Bill before falling off a building.
