For Adult's advices

Anxiety

What might cause?
It might happen to the protester when they are afraid to be arrested.

Symptoms:
- Restlessness
- Irritability
- Difficulty Concentrating

Possible Treatments:
- Psychological Therapies
- Prescribed medication

Depression

What might cause?
It might happen to the protester when they are afraid to be arrested.

Symptoms:
- Feeling tearful
- Feeling guilt-ridden
- Lack of energy

Possible Treatments:
- Exercise/Sport
- Express with people
- Antidepressants
- Combination therapy

PTSD

What might cause?
It might happen to the protester when they have experienced police brutality or sexual brutality. They might also have "survivor's guilt" if escaped.

Symptoms:
- Having flashbacks or nightmares frequently
- Avoidance and emotional numbing
- Irritability

Possible Treatments:
- Psychological Therapies
- Antidepressants
- EMDR
- Group therapy

Phobia

What might cause?
It might happen to the protester when they have experienced police brutality or sexual brutality.

Symptoms:
- Having panic attacks frequently
- Sweating and trembling
- Confusion or disorientation

Possible Treatments:
- Express with people
- Psychological Therapies
- Antidepressants
- Tranquillisers
- Beta-blockers
